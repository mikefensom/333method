Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

The short version: 3-3-3 Method collects no personal data. Everything stays on your device. Nothing is sent to any server.

What data we collect

None. The 3-3-3 Method app does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. All data you enter — your tasks, streak history, reflections, and settings — is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework.

Data storage

All app data is stored locally on your iPhone. It is never sent to any external server, third party, or analytics platform. If you delete the app, all associated data is permanently removed from your device.

iCloud

The app does not currently use iCloud sync. Your data does not leave your device via iCloud or any other cloud service.

Analytics

We do not use any analytics tools, crash reporters, or tracking SDKs. We do not know how you use the app, how often you open it, or what tasks you add.

Notifications

If you grant notification permission, the app schedules local notifications on your device (daily planning reminder, timer completion, optional evening nudge). These notifications are generated entirely on your device and do not involve any external server.

App Store analytics

Apple may collect anonymised usage data through the App Store as part of their standard platform operations. This is governed by Apple's own privacy policy, which can be found at apple.com/privacy.

Children's privacy

The app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. As no data is collected from any user, there are no special provisions required for younger users.

Changes to this policy

If we ever change this privacy policy, we will update the date at the top of this page. As this is a data-free app, significant changes are unlikely.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at hello@333method.app.
